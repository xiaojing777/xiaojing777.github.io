About

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in Statistics at Purdue University, advised by Prof. Fei Xue and Prof. Jianxi Su. Prior to Purdue, I received my bachelor's degree from the School of Economics at Xiamen University in 2022, where I was advised by Prof. Jingyuan Liu.

My research focuses on developing advanced statistical, machine learning, and deep learning methodologies to address challenges in wearable device data, personalized mobile health monitoring, flexible copula modeling, treatment effect heterogeneity (TEH) and accelerated insurance underwriting. Recently, I have been especially interested in AI for Health, particularly in wearable-driven mobile health settings. I am also interested in building AI agents. If you are interested in potential collaboration, please feel free to reach out.
