About

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in Statistics at Purdue University, advised by Prof. Fei Xue and Prof. Jianxi Su. Prior to Purdue, I received my bachelor's degree from the School of Economics at Xiamen University in 2022, where I was advised by Prof. Jingyuan Liu.

My research spans uncertainty quantification for deep learning, large-scale wearable health data, dependence modeling, and LLMs. Methodologically, I develop statistical and machine learning tools for personalized mobile health monitoring, flexible copula modeling, and treatment effect heterogeneity (TEH). Recently, I have been especially interested in AI for Health, particularly in wearable-driven mobile health settings, as well as building AI agents. If you are interested in potential collaboration, please feel free to reach out.
